About Geek Room KRMU

Geek Room KRMU is the official chapter of Geek Room at KR Mangalam University. We're part of India's largest student-led coding network, started by three passionate coders who wanted an open, transparent community focused on sharing ideas and helping others.

75,000+ active coders nationwide
10+ Departments including Web Dev, App Dev, AI/ML, UI/UX, PR & Sponsorship, and more
Regular Events like hackathons, workshops, and competitions
Mentorship & peer learning within KRMU and national chapters
